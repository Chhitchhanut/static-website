Welcome to PNC Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005, in Phnom Penh. PNC offers two IT training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA) and Web Programming (WEP), based on a holistic educative approach including technical and soft skills, and a personal development program.

Sports

At PNC, sports play an important role in student life, promoting health, teamwork, and discipline. Students enjoy football, volleyball, rugby, and pétanque, which build friendships, strengthen community spirit, and encourage cooperation, making sports a valuable part of their education.

FOOTBALL

Football is a popular sport played worldwide, where two teams compete with skill, teamwork, and strategy to score goals and win matches.

VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball is a dynamic sport played on a court, where teams use skill, teamwork, and strategy to hit the ball over the net.

RUGBY

Rugby is a fast, physical sport requiring teamwork, strength, strategy, and endurance, where players aim to score tries and defend passionately.

PETANQUE

Pétanque is a traditional French game where players throw metal balls aiming to land closest to a small wooden target ball.

AEROBIC

Aerobic
At PNC, aerobic activities help students stay active and healthy. They improve heart strength, build endurance, reduce stress, and encourage teamwork, making exercise both enjoyable and beneficial for everyone.
